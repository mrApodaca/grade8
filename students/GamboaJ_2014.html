My life goals

After I graduate high school I will try my hardest to go to college. When I go to college I will go to be a CSI Investigator. I will have to go to college for four years or more. Ever since my teacher showed me a video in class about CSI, I fell in love with it. Thats when I knew I wanted to be a CSI investigator.

I would like to be a CSI Investigator because its cool. I would like to go investigate murder scenes. CSI Investigators investigate crime scenes to try to find out who the murderer is. Theres times that they get the wrong person, but they usually end up finding out who it really was.

CSI Investigators have all kinds of fancy technology that help them figure out clues. When they are looking at pictures of the dead bodies, they compare them to others to see if they have a match, and if they did then that would mean that they have a serial killer out. I would love to learn how to use the technology, to try to find out who was the murderer.
