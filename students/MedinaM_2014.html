My Life Goals

After graduating college, I will become a successful anesthesiologist. An anesthesiologist is a physician trained in anesthesia and perioperative medicine. Anesthesia is a temporary state that causes unconsciousness, loss of memory, and lack of pain and muscle relaxation. Anesthesia is the use of medicine to prevent the feeling of pain or sensation during the operation. There are three types of anesthesia; General anesthesia, Regional anesthesia, and Local anesthesia.

General anesthesia is anesthesia to make the patient completely unconscious. General anesthesia can be given through IV or by inhaling gases or vapors. An example of general anesthesia is when an anesthesiologist puts a patient to sleep to work on major surgery. Major surgery can mean a lot of things, but one example is when somebody has a broken ankle they will need to be given the general anesthesia. This type of anesthesia is used when people need major surgeries.

Regional anesthesia is an anesthetic drug that is injected near nerves, and also numbing larger areas of the body. This type of anesthesia is used on mostly people who are overweight. Regional anesthesia helps the anesthesiologists guide the correct placement of the needle in overweight people. When this type of anesthesia is given the patient is usually asleep before the procedure is finished. Regional anesthesia is more for the overweight people.

Local anesthesia only numbs a small specific part of the body. This type of anesthesia is used when you go for minor surgeries. An example of a minor surgery is when a person has to go for stitches. When local anesthesia is given the person may be awake or asleep. This anesthesia is given certain amounts that the patient can handle or that is needed.

When I graduate college, I will become a successful anesthesiologist. When I was researching all these different types of anesthesia I was very interested. I would want to be an anesthesiologist for many reasons. I would want to experience giving all the types of anesthesia to people. I know I can accomplish if I follow my dreams and don’t let anyone bring me down.
