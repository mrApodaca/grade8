Goal In Life

After graduating from college, I will become successful by being a pediatrician. A pediatrician is a medical practitioner specializing in children and their diseases. To become a pediatrician you need about 4 years of college and 4 years of medical school. After that 8 years of schooling, you will need 3 years of training. A pediatrician takes 11 years of school. As a pediatrician you will always be learning.

A pediatrician makes a great amount of money. The annual salary of a pediatrician is about $175,000.00 a year. But for a monthly salary it is about $14,583.00. Also a pediatrician works a good amount of hours. The job a pediatrician has, goes to hospitals to make rounds and see new infants and sick children. They go into the regular office at 8:00am and gets out around 6:30 pm.

The reasons that I want to be a pediatrician is for so many reasons. One reason is because I want to be in the medical field. Another reason is because I want to be able to help infants, children, and teens. I see a lot of children sick with diseases and I want to help cure them. Also because a pediatrician can practically get a job anywhere. Even if you move to another state, there are plenty of jobs for pediatricians. This is why becoming a pediatrician is such a big goal to me in life.
