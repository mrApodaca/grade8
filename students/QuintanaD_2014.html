Derek Carlos Quintana

After I finish high school I want to go to school to study science.

Family

My family is a funny, wild, and hard working hispanic family.

I live on a ranch in San Acacio. My dad has cows, horses, and pigs. My family manages a lot of land. We grow about 500 acres of alfalfa and graze about 1500 acres. Everyday on Quintana Ranch is amazing and fun.

During the summers we work on the ranch. Summer is my favorite time of year. No matter what we do someone makes it fun. We can be taking down fences, bucking bales, or cutting willows and someone will still make it fun. Although we mess around and have fun, we still work. We do anything from cutting willows and taking down fences to working on vehicles and moving animals.

Interests

My passion is working on the ranch, being social, and playing sports.

I enjoy being active. I like playing sports and running. My favorite sports are basketball and wrestling. I also like to help my dad work on the ranch. He teaches me a lot about the farm and water.
